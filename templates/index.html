WHAT IS CYBER SENTRY?



The application has been developed to identify hate content and further detect its level of toxicity in case it is a hate content. The application firstly identifies if the given content is hate or not.A DistilBert model(we call this BERT-1) in the Flask back-end is used for the same. It has been trained with a wide variety of hate samples including 8 categories:aggressive,attack,non-sarcasm,normal,racism,sarcasm,sexism and toxicity. If the given content is identified to be any form of hate,it will be passed on to the toxicity detection model which in turn is another DistilBert model.This second model(BERT-2 as we call it) classifies the level of toxicity of the hate content in question. The application has been extended to detect hate content and thus its level of toxicity for textual content as well as for images,keeping memes in mind.
